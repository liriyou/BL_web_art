About

In 2056, a huge Enterprise starts a project to enlarge people’s life. The investigation team divides into two groups, one team focus on technology, they are finding a way to mechanize the human body to achieve enteral life. The other team working on changing biological forms like DNA to make the cell more active so the organism recovers better. After years of investigation, the mechanism makes huge progress, 80% of mechanizing is available and starts applying in society. However, the machine comes with risks, there is a chance to become persist vegetative states after the surgery. The bio team lost its position in the competition, there are only a few investigators still working on the project. The chief of the team change the direction to the Chinese legend of fox and capture a five-tail fox. He studies and combines the gene with a test-tube baby of his own. The Tech program feels the threat and tries to kill the test baby.

Charaters

Minami Akane

The Major Characters of the game is a tube-test baby, born are raised by the doctor of the bio enteral program. Have the ability to heal and some kind of heating ability. She had been left in the forest years ago, now lives in the dojo in the forest and trained by a masked man.

god fox

protector of the forest, people pray for happiness and luck. She can easily decide who can come into the forest, or get lost in the forest forever. People say she has 9 tails and can summon the firestorm.

Tengu

A master of a dojo in the forest, always has a Tengu mask on his face, master of the sword. There is no trainee in the dojo, people saw monsters in the dojo before. He finds and gives shelter to the girl he finds under the tree in the forest.

Scenes

The story in base cyberpunk background, the major city is full of skyscrapers. The highest skyscraper is also a simbel of the high class and the high authority
